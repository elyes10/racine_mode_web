our standards

Certifications

ICS Standards

By meeting ICS standards, we demonstrate that sustainability is embedded in every layer of our supply chain. From raw material handling to final assembly, we strive to reduce our environmental impact while maintaining high ethical standards. This certification is a key pillar of our strategy to produce responsibly and build long-term trust with our partners.

Higg Index

Through this module, we ensure that our operations remain transparent and socially responsible. The insights gained allow us to identify areas for development and implement targeted actions that elevate both our workforce wellbeing and operational integrity. This certification reflects our ongoing dedication to responsible and people-centered production.
