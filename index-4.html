about us

26 Years of Textile Passion & Excellence

Founded in 1998, Racine Mode is a family-owned Tunisian manufacturer specializing in mid- to high-end apparel, with a strong focus on babywear in woven fabrics crafted for maximum comfort and safety. With 26 years of expertise, we bring your vision to life through our end-to-end services, from sourcing to planning and logistics.
