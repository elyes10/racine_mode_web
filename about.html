Our Story

Sustainability, Quality, Innovation

Racine Mode was born in 1998 in the coastal city of Monastir, Tunisia, and strategically located in the heart of the Sahel textile industry, with close access to both port and airport facilities. starting as a small, family-run workshop with just a few sewing machines, a handful of talented seamstresses, and one clear purpose: to make beautiful, high-quality garments the right way.

Today, over 26 years later, we've grown into a trusted manufacturing partner for brands across Europe, producing over 1.5 million garments per year. But we haven't forgotten our roots.

Behind every piece we produce is the same dedication, the same hands-on care, and the same belief: when you invest in people, the product speaks for itself.

Our Team

The People Who Make It Happen

Our team isn't just part of the process, they are the process. 85% of our workforce is women, many of whom are heads of households, community leaders, and role models in their own right. Their skill, attention to detail, and work ethic are the backbone of everything we create.

We're proud to offer fair wages, safe working conditions, and long-term career growth, not just jobs. Some team members have been with us for over a decade, passing on their craft to the next generation.
